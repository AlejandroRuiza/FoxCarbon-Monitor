About Us

We are a group of students of CETYS Universidad Mexicali focused on the application, acquisition and diffusion of knowledge in areas mainly about robotics, with the purpose is to contribute to a society through innovation and entrepreneurship of its members.

Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, useful and accurate information on GHG production mainly from human activities. This in order to quantify this information interactively and make the public aware of its carbon footprint.
